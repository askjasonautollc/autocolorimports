Our Mission

To build a smarter, more efficient workflow for the modern body shop by integrating cutting-edge technology with hands-on expertise.

Precision

Our primary goal is to achieve a 95% or greater first-time match rate (Delta E < 1.5). By leveraging AI and expert-validated data, we aim to eliminate costly rework and reduce the time it takes to get a perfect blend, every time.

Value

We believe high-end results shouldn't come with an inaccessible price tag. By partnering with premier global suppliers and optimizing our workflow with technology, we provide professional-grade tints and supplies that deliver exceptional performance without compromising your bottom line.

Innovation

We are a technology company first. From our data-collecting mobile app to our investment in AI-powered formulation, we are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of the automotive paint industry. We're building the tools that will define the future of the modern, efficient body shop.

Our Commitment in Action

Our mission is embodied in our premier product, the Luna Base Paint Line—the foundation of our data-driven future.
