Luna Base: The Smart Paint System

More than just a premium paint line, Luna Base is a complete, technology-driven ecosystem designed for the modern body shop. It combines high-quality, value-based materials with a powerful software suite to deliver unparalleled precision and efficiency.

A Fully Integrated Workflow

The Luna Base system provides everything you need to achieve fast, accurate color matches while reducing waste. Every component is designed to work in harmony, from color capture to final application.

Precision Spectrometer

Capture color data at the source with our high-end, integrated spectrometer for initial formula retrieval.

Automated Mixing Rack

Our software-connected racks ensure your tints are always fresh and ready for precise dispensing.

Integrated Precision Scale

Connect directly to our software for error-free pouring and real-time formula adjustments, guided by our mobile app.

Backed by High-Quality Materials

The Luna Base system is supported by a full range of professional-grade materials, including clears, hardeners, primers, and reducers, all designed to complement our basecoat line and ensure a flawless finish.
