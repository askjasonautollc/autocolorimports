The Technology Behind the Match

Our system is built on a foundation of real-world data, collected by expert painters via our proprietary mobile app.

1. Expert Data Collection

Our ACI Color Match app allows expert technicians to record every detail of their successful manual matches, creating a high-fidelity dataset.

2. AI Model Training

This data is anonymously used to train our next-generation AI models, teaching them the subtle nuances of color formulation that go beyond simple spectro readings.

3. Predictive Formulation

The result is a system that can predict highly accurate formulas for new colors, achieving our goal of a 95%+ first-time match rate.
