How We Achieve the Perfect Match

Our system isn't just about high-quality paint; it's about a smarter process. We combine decades of hands-on expertise with cutting-edge technology to create a system that delivers unparalleled accuracy and value.

Our Data-Driven Workflow

Step 1: The Foundation of Experience

Our system is built on over 50 years of collective, real-world paint mixing experience. We understand that a perfect match often requires a human touch that goes beyond what a machine can see. We decided to capture that intuition.

Step 2: The ACI Color Match App

We developed a proprietary mobile app that allows our expert technicians to record every detail of their successful manual paint matches. Every toner, every gram, and every note is captured, turning decades of experience into structured, analyzable data.

Step 3: AI-Powered Analysis

This high-fidelity data is used to train our AI models. The system learns the subtle adjustments required for complex colors, especially pearls and metallics, and identifies the unique "strength factors" of our Luna Base tints compared to OEM standards.

Step 4: The Predictive Formula

The result is a system that doesn't just look up a standard formula—it predicts a better one. By integrating with industry-leading hardware like X-Rite scanners and precision scales, we can deliver a formula with a 95% or greater first-time match rate (Delta E < 1.5), directly to the painter.

From the Shop Floor to the Cloud

Every piece of data is securely processed and analyzed in our system, building an ever-growing library of color knowledge. This allows us to continuously refine our algorithms and deliver increasingly accurate formulas over time, ensuring our technology always stays ahead of the curve.

Quality, Value, and Speed

This technology-first approach allows us to deliver on three key promises.

Top-Tier Quality

By combining AI precision with high-quality, globally-sourced tints, we deliver OEM-level color accuracy, especially for difficult pearl and metallic finishes.

Exceptional Value

Our efficient, data-driven model reduces waste and costly rework. This allows us to offer a premium paint line at a budget-friendly price point, giving your shop a competitive edge.

Unmatched Speed

Getting the color right the first time dramatically speeds up your workflow. Less time spent on tinting and re-sprays means higher throughput and more satisfied customers.
