Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 12, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Auto Color Imports. This Privacy Policy explains how Auto Color Imports LLC ("ACI", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when using our ACI Color Match application and our website. Our primary goal is to improve color matching technology, and your data is essential to that mission.

Information We Collect

When you record a mix using our application or contact us through our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Non-Personally Identifiable Formula Data: Vehicle Information (YMM), Paint Code, ACI Formula Components (toner codes and weights), and optional notes.
  • Contact Information: Name and email address if you voluntarily provide it through our contact form.

How We Use Your Information

The data you submit is used exclusively for research, development, and business communication to:

  • Analyze paint formulas to identify strength factors and offsets.
  • Train our proprietary AI models to create a predictive color matching system.
  • Improve the accuracy of our paint solutions and enhance our product catalog.
  • Respond to your inquiries sent through our contact form.

Data Sharing and Protection

The formula data we collect is considered proprietary and is not sold, traded, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Your contact information is used solely to communicate with you as requested. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@autocolorimports.com.
